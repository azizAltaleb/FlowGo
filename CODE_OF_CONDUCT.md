# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in the Workflowsa community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- **Respect**: use welcoming and inclusive language.
- **Collaboration**: accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- **Focus**: keep discussions technical, actionable, and aligned with project goals.
- **Responsibility**: report security issues privately and disclose conflicts of interest.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, threats, personal attacks, publishing private information, or sustained disruption of project spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, restrict participation, or ban contributors who violate this code.

Report conduct concerns to `conduct@workflowsa.dev`.
